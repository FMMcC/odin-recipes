Tea

Description

Honestly spent far too long on listing ingredients for other recipes, I've picked this one because it's mich shorter!

That said, I also really love a strong cuppa so it's nice to get that out there

Ingredients

  • A teabag
  • Boiling water
  • A dash of milk (or more, if you're not making it for me)

Steps

  1. Fill the kettle with enough water for your size of cup/mug, and set to boil
  2. Pop a teabag in your cup
  3. Pour your boiling water into the cup until it's almost full
  4. Leave to brew for 3-4 minutes
  5. Remove the teabag, and add a dash of milk (or, if not making it for me, to taste)
