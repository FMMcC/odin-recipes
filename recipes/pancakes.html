Pancakes

Description

This recipe makes up to 3 fluffy american pancakes, with a fruity cinnamon twist. Cobbled together from many different recipes found on the internet.

It's my favourite type of pancake, but my partner prefers crepes - sigh.

Ingredients

  • 135g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking power (or bicarb, whatever is in the cupboard)
  • Half tsp salt
  • 2 tbs sugar (preferably brown but I've found anything works)
  • 1 tbs cinnamon
  • 1 tsp mixed spice
  • So many sultanas (I've never measured them, it's like a layer on the top of the mixture though)
  • 1 large egg
  • 130ml milk
  • 2 tbs oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Sift and combine the dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. In another bowl, gently beat the egg
  3. Add milk to the egg and whisk
  4. Add oil and vanilla to the egg mix, and whisk again
  5. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry, stirring as you go
  6. Once everything is mixed together, spoon a great big dollop onto a hot frying pan and fry for 7 mins, turning once halfway
  7. Either serve immediately or wrap in a tea towel sandwiched between plates to keep warm
  8. Repeat steps 6-7 until you run out of batter
